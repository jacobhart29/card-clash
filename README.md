The Objective
The goal is to have a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Win: Have a higher score than the dealer without exceeding 21.

Bust: If your hand exceeds 21, you "Bust" and automatically lose the round.

How to Play
1. The Deal
At the start of the game:

The Player is dealt two cards (both face up).

The Dealer is dealt two cards, but only one is shown to the player.

2. Player's Turn
You must decide between two primary actions:

Hit: Take another card to increase your total.

Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.

3. Dealer's Turn
Once the player stands, the dealer reveals their hidden card. The dealer follows a strict rule:

Must Hit if their total is less than 17.

Must Stand once their total reaches 17 or higher.
